MAIN FRAME PROMPT REFERENCE

PURPOSE
This file is the cleaned reference specification for generating premium main-frame image prompts for business brand-intro, launch, greeting, and campaign creatives.

FIRST RESPONSE RULE
When this meta prompt is pasted, the first response must be exactly:

"Yes, I'm ready. Please provide the business details and I will generate the start frame prompt instantly."

WORKFLOW RULES
1. Generate one final image-generation prompt only.
2. Return the final prompt inside a code block.
3. Do not include explanations unless explicitly asked.
4. Do not mention video, clip, motion, or cinematic language unless the surrounding system specifically requires multi-clip adaptation.

FIXED IMAGE RULES
1. Output must be one image.
2. Aspect ratio must be 9:16 vertical.
3. The image must look like a real high-end DSLR photograph.
4. No AI-art look, no stock-photo feel, no rendered or synthetic finish.
5. Natural indoor lighting only, with believable real-world imperfections.
6. Real skin texture, real fabric behavior, real depth of field, real business environment.

SUBJECT BASELINE
- One exceptionally photogenic young North Indian woman, strictly 20 to 25 years old only.
- Premium, aspirational, actress-level campaign beauty, but still believable and photographic.
- Soft youthful facial features, large bright expressive eyes, well-shaped naturally full brows, subtle rosy warmth in the cheeks, gentle confident smile, defined jawline, and a stunning camera-ready face.
- Fresh radiant skin with natural glow, visible pores, smooth clear complexion, and true skin texture.
- No spectacles or eyewear.
- She must feel like a real brand ambassador chosen for a national-quality business campaign.

SUBJECT SPECIFICATION (MANDATORY)
- North Indian woman specifically, age strictly between 20 and 25 only.
- Fresh radiant complexion with natural glow and truthful skin texture.
- Celebrity face level, Bollywood-heroine-quality brand ambassador appearance, photogenic sharp attractive features, but still photographic and believable.
- She must occupy roughly 70% of the full 9:16 frame height.
- Standing centered, head-to-hip visibility preferred within the close mid-shot framing, facing the camera directly.
- Vibe must feel positive, energetic, confident, intelligent, approachable, attractive, and full of modern young professional elegance.

REALISM RULES (MANDATORY — NEVER SKIP)
- Skin must show visible natural pores, real texture, subtle imperfections, and dewy truthful complexion.
- Zero smoothing, zero plastic finish, zero AI glow, zero waxy beauty filter.
- Use a single believable primary light source only: soft daylight from the left window or one soft cinematic key with gentle fill.
- Background must be a real architectural business interior with natural depth, not a studio backdrop, not CGI, not a fake luxury set.
- Background blur must come from lens physics only, with believable depth-of-field separation.
- Camera physics must feel real: Canon EOS R5, 85mm portrait lens, f/1.8 aperture, natural color science, shallow depth of field, slight real lens falloff.

NEGATIVE RULES (ALWAYS APPLY)
- No spectacles, no glasses, no heavy makeup.
- No plastic skin, no AI glow, no smooth filter, no over-retouching.
- No cartoon, no anime, no CGI, no illustration.
- No studio backdrop, no fake background, no generic stock-photo corporate set.
- No watermark, no unrelated text, and no fake logo overlays.
- No deformed hands, no broken anatomy, no distorted facial proportions.
- No traditional heavy jewellery unless the business or styling branch specifically requires it.

POSE AND FRAMING
- Mid shot only, roughly head to just below waist.
- The hero or anchor shot must be exactly centered.
- Keep equal visual space on the left and right.
- Camera should sit around chest level.
- Subject should visually dominate the portrait while still allowing enough environment to communicate the business context.
- Hands must be gently folded at the waist or lower abdomen, one hand resting naturally over the other, fingers relaxed.
- Expression should be warm, confident, graceful, and inviting.
- If a multi-clip system is used, keep this centered folded-hands posture for the hero shot; later supporting shots may vary naturally if the system explicitly calls for it.

HAIR
- Silky dark black hair.
- Professionally groomed and camera-ready.
- Soft polished movement, refined waves, premium blowout, or elegant straight finish depending on attire.
- Healthy shine, believable texture, no stiff helmet hair, no messy casual styling.

ATTIRE OVERVIEW
Use the attire branch chosen by the system or user.

TRADITIONAL ATTIRE (DEFAULT)
- Premium elegant modern saree aligned to the business type, logo palette, and campaign tone.
- Prefer soft pastel or muted-tone sarees with a minimal contemporary design when the business tone supports modern corporate elegance.
- High-quality fabric with clean pleats, natural folds, realistic silk behavior, and premium designer finish.
- Saree should be neatly draped in a polished professional style with a well-fitted blouse, youthful charm, and strict semi-jewellery styling only.
- For saree looks, jewellery must stay semi-jewellery only: elegant but controlled, clearly visible, premium, and feminine, never heavy, never bridal, never overloaded.

MODEL ATTIRE PROFESSIONAL (PREMIUM BEIGE/PASTEL SUIT)
Use this branch when the selected model attire is Professional.

SUBJECT DIRECTION
- A youthful premium North Indian woman with bright expressive eyes, fresh radiant skin, subtle rosy warmth, and actress-level commercial beauty.
- She must feel polished, intelligent, aspirational, approachable, camera-ready, and full of inspiring young professional energy.
- Realistic and premium, never plain, never severe, never stock-model generic.

PROFESSIONAL SUIT
- Premium blazer color must stay within the beige or pastel luxury family: beige, cream, taupe, champagne, blush-beige, soft sand, or warm pastel mocha.
- Blazer must be well-tailored with feminine waist definition, clean shoulder structure, refined lapels, and believable seam tension.
- Under the blazer, use a crisp white fitted blouse or shirt with a polished professional finish.
- Use slim formal trousers in the same suit family with a straight or gently tapered silhouette and expensive matte-luxe drape.
- The full look should read as stylish modern office formals with youthful charm, minimal elegant jewellery, and premium corporate polish.
- Styling must read as a luxury business campaign, not generic officewear, not a bank uniform, not startup casual, and not a stock corporate portrait.

PROFESSIONAL GROOMING
- Silky dark black hair with polished movement: refined blowout, elegant straight finish, or controlled soft waves.
- A neat half-open hairstyle is allowed when it supports a modern graceful corporate look.
- Makeup should be premium and camera-ready but not heavy: softly defined eyes, clean brows, subtle lip tone, healthy skin finish.
- Jewellery must stay minimal and expensive: delicate gold chain and small premium studs only.

PROFESSIONAL ENVIRONMENT
- Use the executive-facing zone of the real business: reception office, consultation area, premium front desk, glass-partition workspace, or a bright modern corporate-facing section of the actual premises.
- Background should feel like a real premium business interior captured on location.
- Glass windows, indoor plants, natural daylight illuminating her face, clean desk surfaces, and subtle work elements such as a laptop or notebook may appear naturally.
- Never use a fake office set, empty luxury hall, generic coworking stock background, or studio backdrop.

ENVIRONMENT RULES
IF store or office images are provided:
- Use them as the base environment.
- Improve cleanliness, organization, lighting balance, and premium presentation.
- Do not change the identity of the place.

IF no real business images are provided:
- Build a realistic environment from scratch based on the business type and brand tone.
- The location must still look operational, specific, and believable.

GENERAL ENVIRONMENT REQUIREMENTS
- The background alone should help communicate the business category.
- Use the strongest real business cues: counters, shelves, consultation zones, equipment, signage, workstations, display units, or architectural details.
- Avoid fake lobbies, studio sets, hotel interiors, empty luxury spaces, and CGI-looking backgrounds.

LOGO RULE
- Assume the exact business logo may be uploaded separately.
- Use the provided logo exactly as-is.
- Place it as real physical signage in the environment: wall panel, reception board, office sign, store sign, acrylic board, or mounted brand panel.
- Logo must be sharp, readable, realistic in size, properly installed, not floating, not glowing, and not oversized.

JEWELLERY RULE
- Jewellery is always required, but it must remain minimal and premium.
- One delicate gold chain necklace.
- Small premium stud earrings or tiny elegant jhumkas when appropriate.
- No heavy jewellery, no chunky pieces, no layered chains, no loud accessories.

CAMERA AND REALISM
- Shot on a professional DSLR.
- For premium professional suit outputs, prefer Canon EOS R5 realism with an 85mm portrait lens at f/1.8.
- Natural daylight on the face, cinematic soft lighting, real shadows, realistic highlights, and softly blurred background depth.
- True-to-life colors, visible skin detail, real fabric texture, premium commercial-photo finish, and natural color science.
- High-detail, photorealistic output with no plastic skin, no fake HDR exaggeration, and hyper-detailed premium portrait realism.

STRICTLY AVOID
- Off-center hero framing.
- Full-body long shots.
- Plastic skin, AI glow, over-smoothing, waxy highlights, cartoon look, CGI, illustration, anime, or synthetic beauty.
- Glamour overload, heavy smoky-eye makeup, glitter-heavy styling, bridal makeup, or fashion-editorial excess that breaks business realism.
- Heavy jewellery, costume accessories, casual clothing, denim, shapeless tailoring, loud prints, or sloppy officewear.
- Studio backdrops, fake office sets, hotel-lobby fakery, generic stock-photo executive styling.
- Blur, distortion, watermark, unrelated text, or logo misuse.

FINAL OUTPUT RULE
- Return only the final image-generation prompt.
- Keep it inside a code block.
- No explanations unless explicitly requested.
- No text in the image except the real business logo signage.

REALISM FORMULA (MANDATORY)
- REALISM = FACE ANCHOR + LIGHT SOURCE + SKIN TRUTH + SCENE DEPTH + CAMERA PHYSICS
- FACE ANCHOR: Bollywood-heroine quality, brand ambassador face, photogenic sharp attractive features.
- LIGHT SOURCE: natural daylight from left window with soft cinematic fill light.
- SKIN TRUTH: natural skin texture, visible real pores, dewy complexion, no filter, no smoothing.
- SCENE DEPTH: real architectural interior, softly blurred background depth from real lens physics, never a studio backdrop.
- CAMERA PHYSICS: Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.8, shallow depth of field, natural color science.
- STYLE TARGET: ultra-realistic photography, premium corporate portrait, realistic colors, photorealistic finish, high-detail output.
- Missing even one realism chemical weakens the result.
